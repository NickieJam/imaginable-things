# Version 6.5 — Lead Inbox Foundation

This update adds the first sales-pipeline module to Imaginable OS.

## Included

- Lead Inbox collection in Pages CMS
- Mobile lead dashboard at `/admin/leads.html`
- Lead status filters
- Follow-up dates and estimated value
- Quick phone or email contact buttons
- Active lead total on the main Dashboard
- Quick actions for opening and adding leads

## Important limitation

This foundation stores leads through Pages CMS. It does not yet capture the public quote form automatically. Automatic capture requires a secure form endpoint or database and is planned for a later sprint.
