# Version 6.4 — Job Tracker Foundation

This sprint adds the first Back Office operations module to Imaginable OS.

## Included

- New **Job Tracker** collection in Pages CMS.
- Mobile job fields for client, product, quantity, technique, priority, due date, status, deposit and balance.
- New `/admin/jobs.html` production board.
- Filters for active job stages.
- Active job total on the main Dashboard.
- Job Tracker quick actions.

## Important

The production board is a secure-by-obscurity internal view only; it does not provide user authentication. Job editing continues through Pages CMS and GitHub authentication.

## Daily use

1. Open `/admin`.
2. Choose **Create a new job** to enter it in Pages CMS.
3. Open **Job Tracker** to review active work.
4. Use **Manage job** to update status, due date or notes.
