# Imaginable Things — Version 6.1

## Business Settings

This version creates one central place in Pages CMS for the business information used throughout the website.

Open the admin panel and select **Business Settings, FAQs & Quote**, then edit **Business Settings**.

You can now manage from your phone:

- Brand name and legal name
- Tagline
- Logo
- Phone and WhatsApp
- Business email
- Service area or location
- Optional public address
- Business hours
- Contact note
- Official website
- Browser/Google title and description
- Facebook, Instagram and TikTok links

The website automatically uses these settings in the header, About logo, contact section, footer, browser title, description, favicon and both WhatsApp quote flows.

## Publishing

Pages CMS saves the edit to GitHub. Vercel then republishes the site automatically. Most updates appear within one or two minutes.

## Important

The public address is optional. Leave it blank if customers should not see a home or workshop address.
